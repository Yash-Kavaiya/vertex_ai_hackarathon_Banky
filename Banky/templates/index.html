About Banky

Banky is an AI-powered banking assistant developed during the Google Vertex AI Hackathon. It leverages cutting-edge natural language processing and machine learning models to provide personalized financial advice, customer support, and secure banking services. With Banky, users can seamlessly manage their finances, receive tailored investment recommendations, and enjoy a revolutionized banking experience.
